I came across a clip from Joe Rogan’s podcast featuring Chris Williamson, and it deeply resonated with me.

This "Lonely Chapter" they're talking about - A gap between where you don't want to be, and where you want to be. It just struck me and realized that it's what I've been going through a couple of times recently.

In this phase, I have no idea on how things will turn out. No idea whether there's glory in the end or if I'm just barking up at the wrong tree. People, friends, circles change every time we decide to grow. But not everyone is comfortable with growth, and not everyone will take the same path as yours.

They're right, there'll be temptations of going back. Back to the old ways, the old patterns, back to the comfortable zone where everything just makes sense. 

We better prepare ourselves for this long journey of ours. We may meet new circles in this chapter but as soon as we continue growing, we might walk this Lonely Chapter all over again. And this would be normal.

Cheers to getting comfortable with being uncomfortable.
